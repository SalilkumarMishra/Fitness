Balanced Diet & Nutrition

A proper diet fuels your workouts, supports recovery, and keeps your body strong and energetic every day.

Protein Rich Foods

Essential building blocks for muscle growth and recovery

Eggs

High-quality complete protein source that supports muscle growth, repair, and overall health.

Chicken Breast

Lean protein option ideal for muscle building, low in fat and high in essential amino acids.

Paneer & Tofu

Great vegetarian sources of protein, calcium, and healthy fats for plant-based diets.

Daily Water Intake

Drinking 3–4 liters of water daily improves digestion, boosts metabolism, flushes toxins, and supports muscle recovery.
