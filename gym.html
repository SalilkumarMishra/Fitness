About Gym Training

Understand the benefits and considerations of strength training

Benefits
  • Builds muscle strength and endurance
  • Improves metabolism and stamina
  • Boosts confidence and discipline
  • Supports weight management
Considerations
  • Risk of injury if done incorrectly
  • Requires consistency and effort
  • Muscle soreness in beginners
  • Needs proper rest and nutrition
